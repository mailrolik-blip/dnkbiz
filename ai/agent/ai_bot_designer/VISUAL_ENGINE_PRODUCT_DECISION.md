# Visual Engine Product Decision

## Not

- Generic AI image bot.
- Telegram-only product.
- Canva replacement.
- Predis replacement.
- General-purpose designer.

## Is

- Brand production automation engine.
- Visual Recipe execution.
- Deterministic local rendering.
- Selective AI variable-layer generation.
- Channel-independent DNK module.

## Primary Pilot ICP

1. Agencies managing repetitive branded content.
2. Brands with recurring mascot/character visuals.
3. Sports clubs, schools and leagues with recurring posters.
4. Franchises/networks with repeated localized visual templates.

## Anti-ICP

- Generic creators.
- One-off illustration buyers.
- Users without repeatable visual systems.
- Professional designers seeking unrestricted freeform editing.
